About Me

I’m a PhD student in Canada. I study the cognitive processing of reading. More specifically, the processing at the orthographic and following stages. I design and run behavioural experiments (sometimes with ERP measurement), and use R, Python or Matlab to do data analysis.

I speak Chinese Mandarin (first language), English and a little Japanese (passed JLPT N1). I also love nature and photography. Recently, I got a binocular and a field scope, and started birding. WOW, why didn’t I notice there are so many lovely birds around me?!

This blog is powered by Hugo, and the theme is Hugo-classic with some modifications of mine. In this blog I’d like to share some of my work and photos with you, and hopefully develop some computer skills building it.
